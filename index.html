Kelly is a current first year at the University of California, Riverside in the Pre-Business Program. She later hopes to concentrate in Accounting. As a student, she spends most days attending lectures, studying, and hanging out with friends. She enjoys meeting new people and is eager to learn about the world around her. Upon entering college she has had various experiences both inside and outside of the classroom.

Past jobs that Kelly has had include tutoring, working as a summer camp leader, private violin instructor, and an employee at Its Boba Time. As a summer camp leader, she gained skills in communication as well as leadership. Having to create new activities each day for the kids she has become a faster thinker and writer. Kelly had worked as a private violin instructor for two years. Working with young kids, provided Kelly a new view of the world. Her most recent job, which was at Its Boba Time, involved working as a shift leader, cashier, and barista, As a shift leader, Kelly was in charge of a team of employees in ensuring that everything ran smoothly and customers were satisfied with their drinks. As she oversaw her team, she had to quickly solve problems to avoid delays and dissatisfaction.

As for her personal life, she was born in Torrance, California. She has a twin sister. As a child, she enjoyed playing tennis, playing violin, and baking. On weekends she enjoyed going on family walks and visiting local cafes.
